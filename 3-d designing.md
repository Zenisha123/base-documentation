📖 ***Learning:***

In today’s lesson, we went through the modules of the learning plan. 
It consists of four modules and that day, we went through the first module. 
The first module was: Basic 2D and 3D Designing using CAD Software and we were taught about basic designing in CAD softwares like Fusion360, Tinkercad, and Onshape. We also learned about terms that are related to the CAD softwares. The terms are listed below:
**Additive:** Adding layers to create  
**Subtractive:** Removing parts to create  
**Parametric design:** Way of creating designs where the aspects of designing are controlled by parameters(dimensions, or shapes). For example, if you change one aspect of a design-like the height- the other aspects-like the width- will change automatically to match the new aspect.
